Twilight Pottery

Handmade pottery by Mary Ann Moore

Twilight Pottery is located in rural Chatham County in the beautiful Piedmont area of North Carolina. Started after a long career in education, Twilight Pottery is a small handcrafted business dream come true. Both functional and whimsical items are some of the unique pottery pieces made by hand. My greatest joys come from making something from nothing, exploring the boundaries of clay and glazes.
